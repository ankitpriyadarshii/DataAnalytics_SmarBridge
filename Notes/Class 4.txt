Data Visualisation
-------------------

-Graphical representation of information and data
-By using visual elements like charts, graphs and maps
-Data visualisation tools provide an accessible way to see and understand trends, outliers and pattern in data

Advantage:
-Easily sharing of information
-Interactively explore opportunities
-Visualise patterns and relationships


In Whisker chart, point above whisker is called 'Outliers'

Histograms
